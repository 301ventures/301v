OUR STORY

WHO WE ARE

301 Ventures is a venture capital fund based in College Park, MD that is run by students with both Venture Capital and entrepreneurial experience.

We invest $10K-$50K into companies and passionate entrepreneurs whom we work with for the next 3-5 years. Our investment strategy is simple: we want to back and work with the best.
