Funding

We invest $10K-$50K into companies that we are excited to work with for the next 3-5 years. Our investment strategy is simple, and our term sheets are based off the KISS (Keep It Simple Stupid) model. This means we are honest with you and are working towards the success of your company just as hard as you are.

If you are interested in an investment from Route One Ventures, we recommend preparing the following:

An MVP
Team resumes
A brief 5-10 slide pitch deck
Any relevant financials
A cap table

If you need any help preparing or understanding these resources, please visit our education page or reach out to us Before you reach out for funding, please consider our thesis and requirements to determine if 301 Ventures is a good fit for you and your company.
